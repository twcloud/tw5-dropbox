TiddlyWiki5 in the Sky
using Dropbox beta
(by @Arlen22*)

This app enables you to directly edit TiddlyWiki files stored in your Dropbox. It runs entirely in your browser so your data is never uploaded anywhere except directly to your Dropbox account. **

This site has now been upgraded to the Dropbox V2 API

Full Dropbox Access Apps Folder Access

If you like the idea of having a site like Stack Overflow just for TiddlyWiki questions, click on the banner above to go to the proposal, sign up and verify your email, then click the Commit button. If you are already a Stack Exchange user, this will help even more.

Comments or questions are welcome at https://github.com/twcloud/tw5-dropbox

*Originally built by @Jermolene for TWC, and updated by @Arlen22 to use the Dropbox v2 API.

**The TiddlyWiki files you open have full access to the page, they are not sandboxed in any way.
